# ⚙️ CNC Sand Plotter – Motor Calibration & Motion Tuning (GRBL)

## 📌 Overview
This video demonstrates the calibration and tuning process of the CNC Sand Plotter motors using GRBL firmware. Proper calibration ensures accurate motion and high-quality sand drawings.

---

## 🎥 Video
[Watch the Motor Calibration Demo](https://youtu.be/hECpzw1IvPA)

---

## ⚙️ Calibration Process
- Establishing serial communication with GRBL
- Unlocking the controller
- Testing axis movement (X & Y)
- Adjusting steps per millimeter (steps/mm)
- Verifying direction and positioning
- Evaluating motion smoothness

---

## 🔍 What This Video Shows
- Real-time motor movement testing
- Axis response validation
- Motion accuracy checks
- Basic G-code command execution
- System tuning process

---

## 🎯 Purpose
The goal of this calibration is to ensure:
- Accurate positioning
- Smooth and stable motion
- Correct axis direction
- Reliable system performance

---

## 🚀 Notes
- Calibration is essential before running any complex G-code patterns.
- GRBL firmware is used for motion control.
- A custom `.cmd` script is utilized to simplify communication and testing.
