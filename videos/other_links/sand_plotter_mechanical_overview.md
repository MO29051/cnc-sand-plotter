## ⚙️ System Components
- Frame structure (custom-built)
- CoreXY motion mechanism
- Stepper motors (X-Y axes)
- Belt and pulley system
- Arduino controller running GRBL
- Motor driver (L298N)
- Power supply and wiring setup
